# **App Name**: One Earth Enterprises Portal

## Core Features:

- Company Information Display: Show the 'One Earth Enterprises' company overview, vision, and mission on the website.
- Business Verticals Overview: Display the different business verticals such as IT Services, E-commerce, and Real Estate.
- Contact Form: A form where users can send messages with their name, email, subject, and message.
- "Why One Earth" Section: Highlights the core values, like innovation and trust, demonstrating long-term thinking.
- Company Information Footer: Displays company info, quick links, and contact details in the website footer.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Muted forest green (#3D550C), conveying trustworthiness and growth.
- Background color: Off-white (#F2F0EC), provides a clean, corporate feel.
- Accent color: Subtle gold (#B3A478) for highlights, adding a premium touch.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a modern sans-serif for a corporate, readable feel.
- Clean, component-based structure utilizing generous white space to avoid clutter and provide a premium look.
- Very subtle transitions (e.g., fade-in) to add visual interest without distraction. This may include scroll-based animations.
